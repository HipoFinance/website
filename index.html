How Hipo works

Stake any amount of TON and

earn more than just staking rewards!

1. Stake TON
Earn the highest staking rewards in the market with any amount of TON.
2. Get Liquid hTON
Receive hTON (your liquid staked TON) with no lock-up. Use it across DeFi while still earning rewards.
3. Earn HPO
By staking with Hipo, you become eligible to receive HPO, Hipo’s governance & profit-sharing token.
4. Unstake Anytime
Unstake your TON anytime through Hipo and receive your original TON plus the earned staking rewards.
